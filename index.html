    What is KYUEM Summit?


    KYUEM Summit has been an annual discourse of aspirations & ideas that the KYUEM community has on some of the country’s most pressing issues and in Malaysia’s place in the world. This 10th inaugural addition aims to provide students an opportunity for them to dissect international issues and propose their policy with relevant industry leaders and give an opening for participants to participate in impactful works by NGO’s and industry-based internship.



    This years theme, “Gen Z: Shaping the Voices of Tomorrow Today” highlights how Gen Z is actively redefining the future through their bold action and innovative ideas. By taking control today, they are building a world that are more inclusive and progressive for tomorrow.

    As the current generation, what does Gen Z have to do to stand out? How can they navigate their role in an ever changing world? What can they do to rectify social issues, incorporate unity and build a better, futuristic, diverse and inclusive society? KY Summit 2025 aims to address that.
